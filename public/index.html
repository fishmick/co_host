Intro

Looking for someone to free up some of your valuable time? Well, I can take on some of the mundane tasks of running an Airbnb giving you time time to catch your breath, time to start up new properties, perhaps time spend with the kids or to pursue that passion you have been putting off.

I am located in the Coachella Valley in Southern California and can locally co-host in Palm Springs, Palm Desert, Rancho Mirage, La Quinta, Indio, Coachella, Thermal, Desert Hot Springs, Cathedral City and Indian Wells . Virtual co-hosting is always an option, however local hosting allows you to take full advantage of my skills. Please check my About Me page for a summary of my experience and skill set.

Please contact me to discuss details and pricing options.

    About Me

    Hello, Mike Callahan here in Indio, Ca. USA.

    “I have never managed a short term rental and I am looking to take on my first co-hosting position” does not exactly engender confidence. So why would anyone want to take me on as a co-host? Short answer - fifty years of work and life experience.

    Through these experiences I have developed a few skills. Thirty years of interacting with customers while working as a construction and then service plumber has taught me that how you treat the customer is just as important as the end product. The past three years working as a facilities engineer in a small luxury resort where the guests expect the highest standards of service has elevated my customer service skills.

    Conducting pre-arrival inspections to exacting standards and addressing any issues in the often limited time before the guests arrive continues to be a significant part of my duties at the resort.

    I am very computer literate and many of my positions have involved significant work with office and scheduling software, and I have also completed many online computer courses.

    Also, should you be interested in adding a maintenance contract I have extensive plumbing, electrical, light carpentry and painting skills, and I am a Certified Pool Operator. For further information please check out my LinkedIn page.
